Venture @ NP - E2I NP50 E-Commerce Design Challenge Web Developer

Ngee Ann Polytechnic's E2I Club and NP Co-op jointly organized a NP50 E-Commerce Design Challenge to create a platform for participants to develop a functional and aesthetic e-commerce website for the sale of NP50 t-shirts.

My team has achieved 2nd runner up in this competition with our website. Bright colours were used to show the fun and youthful side of Ngee Ann Polytechnic students.

The website is open to Ngee Ann Polytechnic students, alumnis and staffs to preorder NP50 t-shirts. Preorder is confirmed upon receiving a confirmation email with a preorder slip.

Students will then print out the above receipt to collect their shirts at the designated area.

  • Competition: E2I NP50 E-Commerce Design Challenge
  • Position: 2nd Runner Up
  • Date: November 2013
